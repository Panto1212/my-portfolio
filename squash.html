SQUASH

What is squash?

Squash is basically a game that is played by two people in singles and four people in doubles that involves hitting the squash ball to the wall but also in a way that your opponent doesn't return the ball to the front wall

Types of balls

  • Yellow double dot-extra super slow. This is a competition standard ball
  • Yellow single dot-Super slow. Good for beginners
  • White single dot-Slow. Good for old people
  • Green single dot-Slow
  • Red single dot-Medium
  • Blue single dot-Fast. Best for juniors.
